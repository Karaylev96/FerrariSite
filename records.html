  • Most one-two finishes with the same drivers (Michael Schumacher - Rubens Barrichello): 24
  • Most pole positions (all-time): 207
  • Most WCC points (all-time): 5,618.5
  • Most WDC points (all-time): 6,520.27
  • Most fastest laps (all-time): 229
  • Most consecutive seasons with at least one victory during a season: 20
  • Ferrari is also the most successful F1 engine manufacturer, with 222 wins.
